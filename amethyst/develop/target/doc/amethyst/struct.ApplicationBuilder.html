fn add_barrier(self) -> ApplicationBuilder<'a, 'b, T>

Inserts a barrier which assures that all systems added before the barrier are executed before the ones after this barrier. Does nothing if there were no systems added since the last call to add_barrier(). Thread-local systems are not affected by barriers; they're always executed at the end.
